I Quit

Oct 7th, 2013

I just quit.


I’m still a bit excited. Don’t really know what to do. I just did.
Now the scary and unsettle future is all I have. Despair.
Yet, there is this feeling of relief. Free at last. A bettersweet taste in my month. A little grief in my heart.

To all whom I appreciate: Good bye.
There is no way back, only foward. That’s where I’m looking.
